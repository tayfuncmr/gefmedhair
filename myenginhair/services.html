Our Services

At Gefmed Hair Transplant Clinic, we offer a comprehensive range of hair restoration services designed to meet the unique needs of each patient. Our services include:

  • Hair Transplantation: Utilizing the latest techniques in Follicular Unit Extraction (FUE) and Follicular Unit Transplantation (FUT) to achieve natural and permanent results.
  • Beard Transplantation: Our specialized beard transplant procedures can help you achieve a fuller, thicker beard with natural-looking results.
  • Eyebrow Transplantation: Restore the natural look of your eyebrows with our precise and careful transplant methods, ensuring perfect symmetry and density.
  • PRP Therapy: Enhance hair growth and improve hair density with our Platelet-Rich Plasma (PRP) treatments, a non-surgical option for hair restoration.
  • Scalp Micropigmentation: A cosmetic treatment that simulates the appearance of hair follicles on the scalp, providing a solution for those with thinning hair or bald spots.
  • Hair Loss Consultation: Comprehensive consultations to diagnose the cause of hair loss and develop a personalized treatment plan tailored to your needs.
